Get to know the community

It doesn't matter if you're into food trucks or running, there's a group for you! Hang out at the local Food Truck Festival or sign up for a charity 5K to meet your neighbors!

Food Truck Festival

The Columbus Food Truck Festival is a two-day event featuring the best food trucks in Ohio, entertainment, live music, arts and crafts and family fun for all ages on the downtown riverfront.

Marathons & 5Ks

Hit the trails or partake in one of Columbus' many marathons and 5K runs! Join your fellow Columbusites on winding roads and paths for some great exercise and spectacular views.

Columbus Arts Festival

The Arts Festival features over 200 visual artists, three stages of performances and dozens of food vendors and attracts the nation’s top artists from all over the country.

Arnold Sports Festival

The nation’s largest multi-sport festival brings 20,000+ athletes from 80 nations and 200,000 guests to Columbus to compete in 70 sports ranging from bodybuilding to equestrian.

International Film Festival

Two days of celebrating independent film and honoring makers and creatives around the globe. If you're a film buff, you're not going to want to miss this incredible showcase!
